About Us

Set in Kukës, 37 km from Kalaja Fortress Prizren, Hotel Iseberi offers accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking. The hotel has family rooms.All rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some units at the hotel have a balcony. Mahmet Pasha Hamam is 37 km from Hotel Iseberi, while Korab Mountain is 47 km from the property.

Facilities & Amenities

Non-Smoking Rooms

Free WiFi

Stay connected effortlessly with our complimentary WiFi.

Family Rooms

Experience comfort and space with our family rooms.

Free Parking

Enjoy the convenience of free private parking available on site.

Air Conditioning
